ABOUT.

I'm Jack Graddon.

I'm a high school class of '22 student at Lynden High School in Lynden, Washington. My interest in graphic esign was started in my younger years at my grandparents house, where I had access to a computer with Microsoft Publisher. Publisher isn't exactly the staple of graphic design software, let alone graphic design software at all, but it is what got me interested in design. This then led to various apps related to graphic design and digital art, such as Procreate and Affinity Designer, which I bought for my iPad Pro (2017) from mowing lawns.
